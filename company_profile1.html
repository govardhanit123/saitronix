Company Profile

In 1994, M/s.Saitronix Electro Drives Pvt Ltd. embarked on its journey under the visionary leadership of Shri. Nunna Rama Krishna Rao with a noble mission to serve the Indian Railways. Initially, we started with humble beginnings, focusing on the rehabilitation of RGRs, DBRs, Ohmic shunt resistors, and more for locomotive applications. Recognizing the paramount importance of research and development, our visionary leader harnessed the power of innovation, shaping us into pioneers and frontrunners in the industry.

A company involved in Development , Design, Manitanance and Service.

Innovating and Indigenizing the Future of Highend Technical Products

In 2016, we transformed into M/s.Saitronix Electro Drives Pvt Ltd., cementing our commitment to professionalism and transparency. Today, at SEDPL, our unwavering commitment to quality and excellence is reflected in our products, our people, and our approach to business. We adhere to the highest standards, infusing quality into every aspect of our end-to-end manufacturing processes. Professionalism at all levels and strict adherence to the ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System underscore our dedication to delivering exceptional results.

VISION

To deliver exceptional, cutting-edge, multi-domain technical products across a range of industries.Our commitment to innovation and quality drives us to redefine industry standards.

MISSION 2026

To expand our presence in EMU, Vande Bharat, Coaching, and Electrification segments within the railway industry.To explore new horizons by diversifying into Building Management Systems.

FUTURE ASPECTS

For our future we trust in the commitment as well as the high motivation of our employees. On this basis we want to do justice to the demands of our customers and we want to guarantee our position as one of the top-Manufacture of Highend Industrial Products.
