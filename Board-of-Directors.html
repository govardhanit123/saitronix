Board Of Directors

Board Of Directors

Our board of directors is a blend of seasoned industry experts, each contributing their wealth of knowledge and experience to guide M/s.Saitronix Electro Drives Pvt Ltd. toward new heights of success. Under the visionary leadership of Shri. Nunna Rama Krishna Rao, our founder, and the driving force behind our company's inception, our board collectively steers our organization toward innovation and excellence in the locomotive industry. Their commitment to research and development has solidified our reputation as pioneers and frontrunners in the field. Together, they shape our future, ensuring we remain at the forefront of the industry, delivering cutting-edge solutions for the Indian Railways and beyond.

N AGNI TEJA

CEO & Director

  • PROFILE

    N. Agni Teja has been with the company since 2010 and assumed the role of CEO in 2021. With a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Vidya Jyothi Institute of Technology and an M.Tech in VLSI & Embedded Systems from SEER-JNTU University, he initially contributed to innovation products within the firm. Over time, he transitioned into managing marketing activities, customer interactions on technical and commercial matters, and now, as CEO, he oversees the overall functioning of all departments while dedicating significant time to R&D for new product designs.

N. KALYAN CHAKRAVARTHY

Director

  • PROFILE

    Kalyan has been part of our company since 2014, holding a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from NNRG educational institutions and an M.S in Advance industrial manufacturing systems from Kingston University London . He initially focused on mechanical product design, later taking charge of the entire spectrum of business operations, from production to final product delivery.

Y. NARENDRA BABU

(Founder of KDS Enterprises)

Mentor & Director

  • PROFILE

    Y. Narendra Babu is the founder of KDS Enterprises, a precision sheet metal fabrication unit with diverse manufacturing capabilities, ranging from prototypes to production quantities. Established in 1988 by Narendra, a Engineering Graduate from Hyderabad, KDS Enterprises has a rich history of precision fabrication. He has vast experience in mechanical design and sheet metal fabrication design.

Boppana V Ramabrahmam

(Former Chief Scientist, CSIR, GoI)

Mentor & Director

  • PROFILE

    Boppana V Ramabrahmam Former Chief Scientist, CSIR, Government of India Ramabrahmam is an outstanding Scientist, diligent CEO/Director, Successful Consultant with over 40 years of diverse experience mainly in Chemical, Cement and Leather Sectors. His expertise covers Process development, Plant design and economics, Technology development and transfer, Industrial safety and Chemical risk management, Environment and establishment of ECO Industrial parks/estates. He is a B.Tech Graduate of the Andhra University in Chemical Engineering and MBA from Annamalai University and received Project Management certificate from Strathclyde Graduate Business School, UK and certified ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 Auditor. More than 100 companies (SME’s and fortune 500) have availed Ramabrahmam’s expertise and guidance and successfully implemented UNIDO Cleaner technologies in India and Abroad. He has published over 50 technical papers, holds 5 Patents, authored 2 books and developed over 10 technologies. He is honorary faculty of Anna University, RLI and Anna Institute of Management, Chennai. He has won many awards. He is past chairman of Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers-CRC besides being Director/Expert Member on the various State and Central Government and Private boards/bodies/institutions. Ramabrahmam’s contributions have led to remarkable results, including Job creation, Pollution reduction, Improved Safety, Quality and Productivity and Foreign exchange generation.
